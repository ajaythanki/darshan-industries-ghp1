High Alumina Calcined Bauxite

Premium quality calcined bauxite available in lumps, grains, and fines for diverse industrial applications

Advanced Processing Technology

Our calcined bauxite is processed through a sophisticated 4-step manufacturing process that ensures superior quality and consistency. From sourcing high-grade raw bauxite to precision grinding and grading, we maintain strict quality control throughout.

We offer calcined bauxite in three primary forms: lumps (20-100mm), precision-graded grains (0-1mm, 1-3mm, 3-5mm), and fine powders (80-180 mesh) to meet diverse industrial requirements.
