Premium Manufacturing Process

Our refractory cements are manufactured through a comprehensive 8-step process that ensures superior quality and consistency. From sourcing high-grade raw bauxite and limestone to final packaging, every step is carefully monitored and controlled.

The process includes individual processing of raw materials, precise mixing, nodulization, high-temperature calcination, ball milling crushing, and rigorous quality control testing to meet international standards.
