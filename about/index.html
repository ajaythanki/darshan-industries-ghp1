Our Story

Darshan Industries Lab & Chemical is a premier manufacturer of industrial refractory materials, specializing in high-quality refractory cement, castables, and calcined bauxite since 2008.

With over 16 years of combined industry experience, we have established ourselves as a trusted manufacturer and exporter of high-quality refractory materials. Our commitment to innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction has made us a preferred partner for industries worldwide.

Located in the industrial hub of Porbandar, Gujarat, our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility is equipped with advanced technology and staffed by skilled professionals who ensure that every product meets the highest international standards.
